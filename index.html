Gameplay Instructions
Psychic-Game let's connect with your inner powers to guess the computer's chosen letter. Each game provides you with 9 chances to choose the computer's letter. Use the keyboard to pick your letters. Picked letters will show to prevent you from picking the same letter more than once. If you pick the same letter as the computer before your 9 guess are up, you win! Otherwise, you lose. After each game, win or lose, the computer will pick a new letter and your number of chances to guess will automatically reset.
